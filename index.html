Cinderella

The enchanting Brothers Grimm fairytale. Performed by Frankfurt Youth Ballet.

Swan Lake

The story of a princess turned into a swan by an evil sorcerer's curse. Performed by the Royal Ballet. Composed by Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky.

La Bayadère

The story of the temple dancer Nikiya and the warrior Solor. Performed by the Imperial Ballet. Music composed by Ludwig Minkus.

The Nativity

A play about the birth of Jesus Christ. Performed by Theatergruppe Frankfurt

Lady Windermere's Fan

A Play about a Good Woman. Performed by Theatergruppe Frankfurt. Written by Oscar Wilde

Much Ado About Nothing

A comedy about gossip, rumour and overhearing. Performed by Theatergruppe Frankfurt. Written by William Shakespeare.

A Tribute to Clara Schumann

Frankfurt Youth Orchestra perform a selection of works by the classical composer and pianist Clara Schumann.

Classical Evening

Frankfurt Orchestra play a selected mix of works by both male and female classical composers from down through the ages.

Competition Time

Have your say on Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra vs. London Symphony Orchestra. Which orchestra appears more to you.

Gospel Choir

Frankfurt Gospel Choir performing songs from the soundtrack to the movie Sister Act. Music by Alan Menken and Lyrics by Glenn Slater.

Monteverdi Choir

This specialist Baroque ensemble performs an extensive array from the Renaissance to classical music of the 20th century.

Wells Cathedral Choir

Join this youth choir as they Christmas carol their way through the beginning of the New Year.

Cats the Musical

This musical composed by Andrew Lloyd Webber, tells the story of a tribe of cats called the jellicles.

Les Misérables

A musical adaptation of a novel by Victor Hugo. The story of Jean Valjean a French peasant, and his desire for redemption after serving nineteen years in jail for having stolen a loaf of bread for his sister's starving child.

Phantom of the Opera

A novel by Gaston Leroux adapted to a musical by Andrew Lloyd Webber. The Palais Garnier is believed to be haunted by the Phantom. This is the story of Erik the son of a construction business owner, deformed from birth.
